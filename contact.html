COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the eligibility check work?

Our online eligibility checker is a quick, confidential assessment that takes about 2 minutes. You'll answer questions about your health, medical history, and weight goals. Our UK-registered independent prescribers will then review your answers to determine if prescription weight loss treatment is safe and suitable for you.

What weight loss treatments are available?

Superior Pharmacy offers UK-licensed prescription weight loss medications like Wegovy (semaglutide) and Mounjaro (tirzepatide). These are GLP-1 agonists proven to help with significant weight loss. Our prescribers will recommend the best option for you based on your individual profile.

What is the consultation process like?

Our consultations are 100% online. After completing your eligibility check, our independent prescribers will review your information. If approved, your prescription is issued. There are no video calls or in-person appointments required, making the process convenient and discreet.

How is delivery handled, and is it discreet?

Medication is dispatched same-day (if approved before 2pm Mon-Fri) via tracked Royal Mail. All packaging is discreet and unmarked, ensuring your privacy. Delivery typically occurs within 48 hours to any UK address.
