FAQs

How much will I actually save by switching to Superior Pharmacy?

Wegovy costs from £149.99 per month with Superior Pharmacy, which includes your prescription from our independent prescribers, genuine UK-licensed medication, and free next-day delivery. Many private clinics charge £250-350+ per month for the same medication. You'll save £100-200 per month compared to typical private clinic prices, with no hidden fees or long-term contracts required.

How quickly will I get my medication compared to high street pharmacies?

Your prescription is reviewed within 4 hours by our UK-registered independent prescribers. If approved, medication is dispatched same-day via tracked Royal Mail for delivery within 48 hours. High street pharmacies often have stock issues with Wegovy and may require multiple visits. With Superior Pharmacy, everything is delivered discreetly to your door.

What happens when I need my prescription renewed?

We offer automatic monthly refills so you never run out. Before each refill, our prescribers review your progress and ensure the medication is still suitable. You can pause or cancel at any time with no penalties. We'll send you reminders before each refill is processed, and you can adjust your delivery schedule anytime through your account.

How much weight will I lose with Wegovy?

Clinical trials show Wegovy (semaglutide) helps patients lose an average of 12-15% of their body weight over 12 months. Individual results vary based on starting weight, adherence to treatment, and lifestyle factors. Our patients typically see noticeable results within 4-8 weeks. Your prescriber will monitor your progress and adjust dosage as needed to optimise results safely.

What if Superior Pharmacy isn't right for me?

You can cancel anytime with no penalties or long-term commitments. If our prescribers determine Wegovy isn't safe or suitable for you during the initial assessment, we'll provide a full refund. We're here to support your weight loss journey, and that means being honest about whether our service is the right fit for your needs.

Is Wegovy safe and regulated?

Yes. Wegovy is approved by the MHRA for weight management in adults with obesity. Superior Pharmacy is fully registered with the General Pharmaceutical Council (GPhC). All prescriptions are issued by UK-registered independent prescribers (Raman Sangha GPhC 2085763 or Resham Sira GPhC 2086835). We only dispense genuine, UK-licensed Wegovy from approved suppliers.
