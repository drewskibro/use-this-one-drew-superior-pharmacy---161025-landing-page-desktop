GOT QUESTIONS?

Treatment FAQs

How quickly will I receive my medication?

Your medication is delivered within 24 hours (if eligible following consultation). All deliveries are sent via Royal Mail Tracked 24, which aims to deliver within 24 hours (though this is not guaranteed). All medication arrives in discreet packaging that maintains the cold chain.

Do I need to choose a treatment before my assessment?

No. You can indicate a preference during your assessment, but our prescribers will review your medical history and recommend the most appropriate treatment for you. They'll consider factors like your health conditions, medications, weight loss goals, and lifestyle.

Can I switch between treatments?

Yes. If you experience side effects or want to try a different medication, contact our prescriber team. They'll review your progress and can switch you to an alternative treatment if clinically appropriate. There's no penalty for switching.

Are these medications safe?

All medications we prescribe are approved by the MHRA for weight management and have been extensively tested in clinical trials. Our prescribers only prescribe when it's safe and suitable based on your medical history. We monitor your progress and are available for support throughout your treatment.

What if I'm not approved for medication?

If our prescribers determine that prescription weight loss medication isn't safe or suitable for you based on your medical history, we'll explain why and provide a full refund. Your safety is our priority, and we only prescribe when clinically appropriate.
