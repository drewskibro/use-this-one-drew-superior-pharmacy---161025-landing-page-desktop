GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our online weight loss service.

How much does the programme cost?

Wegovy costs from £199 per month. Mounjaro costs from £229 per month. This includes your prescription from our independent prescribers, genuine medication, and free delivery within 48 hours. No hidden fees or long-term contracts.

How does the online prescription process work?

Complete eligibility checker – pay £10 to book video consultation – if approved, prescription issued instantly

Am I suitable for prescription weight loss medication?

You may be suitable if you have a BMI of 30 or above, or 27 or above with a weight-related health condition (such as type 2 diabetes, high blood pressure, or sleep apnoea). Our prescribers assess your full medical history during the online assessment to ensure medication is safe for you.

Which medication is better - Wegovy or Mounjaro?

Both are effective GLP-1 medications. Wegovy (semaglutide) shows average 15% weight loss in clinical trials. Mounjaro (tirzepatide) shows average 20% weight loss. Our prescribers will recommend the most suitable option based on your health profile, medical history, and goals during your assessment.

How quickly will my medication arrive?

Delivered within 48 hours via Royal Mail Tracked 24 in discreet packaging

What are the side effects?

Common side effects include nausea, constipation, and reduced appetite, especially in the first few weeks. These usually improve as your body adjusts. We start you on a low dose and increase gradually to minimise side effects. Our pharmacist team is available via phone, email, or WhatsApp for support and guidance.

Do I need to diet and exercise?

The medication significantly reduces hunger, making it easier to eat less naturally. We provide nutrition guidance, but there's no strict diet plan. Regular movement is encouraged for overall health, but intense exercise isn't required. Many patients naturally become more active as they lose weight and gain energy.

How long will I need to take the medication?

Most patients continue for 6-18 months to reach their goal weight. Some continue longer for maintenance. There's no fixed timeline. Monthly refills are available, and you can pause or stop at any time. We'll support you in developing a maintenance plan when you're ready.

Is this service regulated?

Yes. Superior Pharmacy is fully licensed and regulated by the General Pharmaceutical Council (GPhC). All prescriptions are issued by UK-registered independent prescribers. We only dispense genuine, UK-licensed Wegovy and Mounjaro from approved suppliers.

What if I'm not approved for medication?

If our prescribers determine medication isn't safe or suitable for you based on your medical history, we'll explain why and provide a full refund. Your safety is our priority, and we only prescribe when clinically appropriate.

How often will I need to check in with the pharmacist?

You'll have clinical oversight by prescribing pharmacists every step of the way
