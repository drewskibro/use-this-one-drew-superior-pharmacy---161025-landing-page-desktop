Superior Pharmacy was founded in 2025 with one clear mission – to raise the standard of online pharmacy care.

As one of the newest providers to be approved, we've built our service to meet the latest and highest regulatory standards from day one. Where many established companies are still adapting to updated safety requirements, we've made patient safety and clinical excellence the foundation of everything we do.

Our journey began with Prescribe Me, a CQC-registered prescribing service that has supported over 100 independent pharmacies across the UK. Through this work, we gained valuable insight into what patients truly want – a safe, reliable, and supportive service that puts their wellbeing first.

We also noticed that many patients were paying premium prices for access to nutrition support, tracking apps, and ongoing coaching. We believe that comprehensive care shouldn't be a luxury – which is why every Superior Pharmacy patient receives these tools as standard, at no extra cost.

As one of the few UK online pharmacies dedicated solely to weight-loss treatment and support, our focus is clear: expert guidance, safe prescribing, and an approach that helps every patient achieve long-term results, not quick fixes.

HOW WE'RE DIFFERENT

What makes us different

01

Built for Today's Highest Standards

As a newly approved pharmacy in 2025, we've been held to the most up-to-date regulatory requirements from the start. Many older providers are still adapting to new safety measures — but we designed every part of our service to meet them from day one. 02

Complete Support, Included as Standard

We believe lasting results come from ongoing guidance — not just prescriptions. That's why every patient receives access to our exclusive app for progress tracking, nutrition support, and habit monitoring at no extra cost. While other providers charge premium prices for these tools, we've made them a core part of your care.

03

Specialists in Weight Management

Unlike general online pharmacies offering multiple treatment areas, we focus solely on safe and effective weight-loss care. Our dedicated team and prescribing service mean you'll receive expert advice and tailored support at every step — from selecting the right treatment to building healthy, sustainable habits that last.
